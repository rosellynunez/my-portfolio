2. Optimizing Design Graphics

I received the graphic assets from the design team, and they were, shall we say, generously sized. Way too big for the web. I adjusted image dimensions to align with the design specifications, preventing unnecessary bandwidth consumption. Of course, it had to look good everywhere, so I optimized for both desktop and mobile. And to really boost speed, I converted everything to WebP. Ultimately, this graphic optimization is vital for web performance, improving load times, SEO, and accessibility.
