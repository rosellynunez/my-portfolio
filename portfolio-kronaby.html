Kronaby Web Page Redesing

Kronaby, a brand within the Festina Group, a Spanish watchmaker with over a century of experience and a global reach spanning 90 countries, is known for its hybrid watches that seamlessly blend the latest technology with a design synonymous with Swedish minimalism. Recognizing the need for a modern online presence, Kronaby underwent a website redesign.

About

The project aimed to enhance the ecommerce experience and improve brand consistency across the group. The objective was to enhance the ecommerce experience, strengthen brand consistency across the group, and improve operational efficiency.
By migrating from PrestaShop to Festina's digital ecosystem, developed under Bootstrap guidelines, we addressed the challenges of managing and updating multiple brands independently, enabling more efficient and consistent brand management.

Go Kronaby project alive

How I helped

I led the user experience and the user interface for the Kronaby website redesign. My responsibilities included user research, competitive analysis, brand-driven design, wireframing, prototyping, and close collaboration with developers. I ensured a smooth transition from design to development through tools like Figma and Storybook.

1. UX Decisions at Kronaby

When I embarked on this project, I understood that while a UI redesign was possible, it was important to carefully consider the existing app's design and ensure alignment with the brand guidelines. Also, the Kronaby webpage needed to be integrated into the Festina ecosystem. This meant adhering to certain constraints, such as maintaining consistency in the product grid and product pages across all brands due to technical limitations.
Therefore, my focus was on preserving what was working effectively while significantly improving areas that required enhancement. Unfortunately, the original webpage lacked integration with Google Analytics or any other performance tracking tools. The only available data indicated a lack of online purchases, prompting my initial steps to focus on:

  • Investigation: Interviews with users provided insights into their expectations, and on the other hand, interviews with stakeholders and the team that was working with this brans since it was born. This both points of view helped me to align the website with what mattered most.

  • Benchmarking: We benchmarked competitor websites, particularly in the premium watch and tech sectors, to gather ideas and ensure our ecommerce strategy was optimized for user engagement and conversion.

  • Simplifying content structure: One major design improvement was simplifying the site's typography and content hierarchy. The previous design had eight different heading styles, twelve subtitles, and five paragraph styles. I reduced these to four headings, four subtitles, and five paragraph styles, creating a more cohesive and readable experience without compromising on brand expression.

3. Hypothesis and Findings

If we provide users with quick navigation on the homepage and expose high-value features, we can improve user engagement. By presenting content in a less overwhelming manner and explainining the meaning and features of these watches, we can boost user engagement.
On the other hand, it was important to work in a seo strategy align with the content. This way, we could revitalize a site that was previously underperforming and inactive, generating minimal revenue.

4. Structure

I began with low-fidelity wireframes to establish the key user flows. The focus was on explain the Kronaby's hybrid watches are and creating a seamless way to process to see the watches features. The initial wireframes explored the structure of the home page, product page, and the landing pages. Once the wireframes were validated, we moved to high-fidelity designs.

5. UI Decisions: Colors, Typography, and Aesthetics

The color scheme was carefully crafted to reflect Kronaby's premium and modern identity. Neutral tones were paired with accent colors that highlighted the brand's innovative character.

For typography, the goal was to achieve an elegant yet readable style that complemented the overall aesthetic without overwhelming users. While the Kronaby app originally used Avenir Next for titles, buttons, and headers, and Minion for body text, these fonts were not part of Google Fonts and posed performance challenges. To address this, I chose DM Sans‚Äîa minimalist font that embodies Kronaby's refined personality while maintaining clarity and readability. This typography selection ensured that text was legible across various screen sizes, delivering a seamless user experience.

The color palette was central to the design's success. The primary palette, anchored by mint-cool-500 (#417178), established a sophisticated foundation. The accent color, brown-500 (#785954), was selected as its complement. A secondary palette, derived from the primary colors by adjusting their saturation and luminosity, was developed to provide visual interest and variety.
In addition to these, semantic colors played an important role. The final palette is both elegant and effective.

The grill page uses its primary color mint-cool-100 on the back layer, and the primary mint-cool-900 for typography and iconography. A secondary color brown-vivid-500 is applied as an accent to the like iteractive icon.

6. Collaboration with the Marketing and Strategy Team

In this project, I worked closely with both the Spain-based marketing team and strategic teams in Sweden, ensuring that the Kronaby brand identity was perfectly translated into the digital experience. It was important working with Sweden team because They manage the app Kroaby project. We conducted bi-weekly meetings to align on strategy, marketing goals, and design direction, allowing us to iterate effectively and ensure that every aspect of the project was on track.

7. Adherence to Bootstrap Guidelines and components

We kept the Kronaby redesign within the Bootstrap framework, aligning with the future move to Tailwind CSS across the Festina ecosystem. This helped maintain consistency and made it easier to integrate with other Festina brands down the line. To save time and effort, we reused components whenever possible. We only created new ones when absolutely necessary, always thinking about how they could be useful for other projects within the Festina family.
Since Kronaby offers hybrid connected watches, which can be a bit confusing for some, we needed a clear way to explain their features. There wasn't an existing component that could do this, so I designed a new one specifically for that purpose. I believe this component will be valuable for other Festina brands as well, especially as they start to incorporate more connected features into their watches.
