About Kronaby

Kronaby is part of Festina Group, a global leader in the watch industry. Festina, a Spanish-based company, manages a portfolio of high-end watch brands, including Lotus, Jaguar, Candino, and, of course, Kronaby. With over 100 years of experience, Festina Group sells millions of watches annually, with an international presence in over 90 countries.
Kronaby's hybrid watches combine the latest technology with a design synonymous with Swedish minimalism.

3. UX Decisions at Kronaby: Research and Best Practices

The redesign of Kronaby's website began with a comprehensive user research phase:

  • Preliminary research: Analyzing data from Google Analytics and heatmaps helped us identify navigation pain points and understand user behavior.

  • User interviews: Qualitative interviews with selected users provided insights into their expectations, helping us align the website with what mattered most to them.

  • Personas and scenarios: Based on these interviews, we developed user personas and scenarios to guide our design decisions.

  • Benchmarking: We benchmarked competitor websites, particularly in the premium watch and tech sectors, to gather ideas and ensure our ecommerce strategy was optimized for user engagement and conversion.

  • Ecommerce optimization: We streamlined the checkout process, optimized product page layouts, and improved overall navigation to reduce friction and enhance user satisfaction.


  • Simplifying content structure: One major design improvement was simplifying the siteâ€™s typography and content hierarchy.
